Droom

Droom is a job seeking/ hiring platform that resembles Indeed. This was part of a team effort in which I was responsible for developing the functionality for users to sign up, display open job positions or people looking for jobs, save people or jobs to a profile page, and update a profile page while consuming a API made by a teammate.
